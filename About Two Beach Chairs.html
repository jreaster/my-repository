"Two Beach Chairs"

These two beach chairs presented a photo that Anita could not resist. It was taken on the beach at Palmetto Dunes, Hilton Head Island, South Carolina on 7/1/2018. The photo was printed on an Epson P-800 with image size of 4.5 inches wide and 6 inches high with a resolution of 213 pixels/inch. It is framed in a black aluminum frame (8x9.5 inches) with a 1.75-inch white mat.
